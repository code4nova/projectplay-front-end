Why Play

Big kids, little kids, playing is good for all kids! Playing is fun, but did you know it’s also a really important way for kids to learn, develop and stay healthy?

Planying helps children learn


  • physical abilities – like balancing blocks and running on the playground
  • cooperation and social skills – like learning to share with other kids in a play group
  • cognitive skills – like math and problem solving by counting how many red birds are in the park
  • imagination and creativity – by guessing what shape a cloud looks like or hopping like a bunny
  • self-esteem – by being praised for trying something new, like going down a slide

Playing can help children be healthy and active.

Kids who play outside are happier, healthier, and stronger! Studies show that children who play outdoors regularly:

  • Become fitter and leaner
  • Develop stronger immune systems
  • Have more active imaginations
  • Have lower stress levels
  • Play more creatively
